About Me

Drummer, Teacher, Performer

My Journey with Drums

I have been playing drums since I was around 3 years old and have over 5 years of professional experience. My passion for rhythm and percussion has taken me from small local venues to prestigious theaters like the Moore and Paramount in Seattle.

Currently enrolled at the University of Oregon studying jazz drumset under professor Ken Maestrogiovanni, I'm constantly refining my skills through academic study and practical experience with various ensembles within the school and outside.

Whether you're looking for a drummer for your next tour, studio session, or seeking to improve your own drumming skills, I bring enthusiasm, professionalism, and years of experience to the table.

Teaching Philosophy

Personalized Approach

I believe every student is unique and requires a customized teaching approach. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine specific techniques, I tailor my lessons to fit your goals and learning style.

Building Strong Foundations

My teaching focuses on developing proper technique from the beginning, Focusing on rudimental fundamentals, stick technique, and more, while still maintaining a fun and engaging approach.

Music Theory Integration

Understanding the why behind the what is crucial. I incorporate relevant music theory concepts that help you become not just a drummer, but a complete musician.
