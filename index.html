About Me

Hi, I'm Nate! I have been playing drums since I was around 3 years old and have over 5 years of professional experience, I am available for touring, studio sessions, and drum lessons for beginners to intermediate. I am currently enrolled at the University of Oregon studying jazz drumset and am involved in numerous ensembles.
